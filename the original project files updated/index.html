Cookie Policy

Last updated: August 3, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how CoinTrade.ca (“we”, “us”, or “our”) uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit or use our website.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They help websites remember your actions and preferences (such as login, language, and display preferences) over a period of time, so you don’t have to re-enter them whenever you visit the site.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Necessary Cookies: These cookies are essential for the website to function properly. They enable core functionalities such as security, network management, and accessibility.
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies collect information about how visitors use our website (e.g., pages visited, time spent) to help us improve site performance.
  • Functional Cookies: These cookies remember your preferences (e.g., language or region) and provide enhanced, personalized features.
  • Advertising and Targeting Cookies: These cookies are used to deliver relevant advertisements and marketing messages tailored to your interests.
3. Third-Party Cookies

We may allow third-party service providers (such as analytics and advertising partners) to place cookies on your device when you use our website. These third parties may collect information about your online activities across different websites.

4. Managing Cookies

Most web browsers automatically accept cookies by default. However, you can usually modify your browser settings to:

  • Block or delete cookies.
  • Receive notifications when you receive a new cookie.
  • Disable cookies altogether.

Please note that if you disable or block certain cookies, some parts of our website may not function properly or offer full functionality.

5. More Information

For more details on cookies and how to manage them, please visit www.aboutcookies.org or consult your browser’s help section.
